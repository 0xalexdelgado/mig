About Mozilla InvestiGator

MIG was created in 2013 by Julien Vehent, in the Operations Security team at Mozilla. It is developed as a fully open source project, published under the Mozilla Public License, and welcoming contributors from everywhere.

MIG is provided free of charge, and without commercial support. However, feel free to contact the OpSec group at Mozilla via their public mailing list if you have any question or comment.
